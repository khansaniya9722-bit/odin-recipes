lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy bechamel, and melted cheese. It is baked until golden and bubbly

It is a comforting and hearty meal that is loved all over the world and is perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

ingredients

  • lasagna sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cheese (mozzarella & parmesan)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & pepper

steps

  1. Cook the ground beef with onion and garlic.
  2. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Boil lasagna sheets until soft.
  4. Layer sauce, pasta, and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers until dish is full.
  6. Bake at 180°C for 30–40 minutes.
  7. Let it cool slightly before serving.
