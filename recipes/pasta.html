Pasta

Description

Pasta is a classic Italian dish made with noodles and delicious sauces like tomato or cream-based sauces.

Ingredients

  • Pasta noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water.
  2. Prepare sauce in a pan.
  3. Mix pasta with sauce.
  4. Serve hot with cheese.
