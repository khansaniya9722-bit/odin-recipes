Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with dough, tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Vegetables or toppings
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Prepare pizza dough.
  2. Spread sauce and add toppings.
  3. Bake in oven until golden.
  4. Slice and serve hot.
